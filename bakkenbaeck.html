At a digital studio, Backen & Bæck, I worked in both product and branding teams in their Oslo, Norway office. I designed ChessVision as an inspiration from Magnus Carlsen, a Norwegian Chess Master (and was also a great excuse to learn computer vision and leave unfinished chess games in the office).

I also worked with DX' CEO and our UX Lead Silja, to brand and design their smart scheduling system as a part of their cinema backoffice solutions. Lastly, I joined the branding team on creating assets such as iconography for a telecom company, Millom, and brainstorming for a design-led angel investor's new capital, Form Capital.
